Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

I'm having technical issues with the app. Who should I contact?

For technical support, please select "Technical Support" in the contact form or email directly to support@farm2market.cm with details about the issue you're experiencing.

How can I partner with Farm2Market?

We're always open to partnerships that align with our mission. Please select "Partnership Opportunities" in the contact form and provide details about your organization and partnership ideas.
