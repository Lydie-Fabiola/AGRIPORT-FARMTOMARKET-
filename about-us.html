About AGRIPORT

Transforming agricultural commerce in Cameroon

Our Mission

AGRIPORT was founded in 2025 with a simple but powerful mission: to create a direct connection between farmers and buyers, eliminating unnecessary middlemen and creating a more efficient, fair, and transparent agricultural marketplace.

We believe that farmers deserve fair compensation for their hard work, and buyers deserve access to fresh, local produce at reasonable prices. By bridging this gap, we're helping to build a more sustainable food system in Cameroon and beyond.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every farmer in Cameroon has access to digital tools that expand their market reach, increase their income, and improve their livelihoods.

By 2030, we aim to connect 100,000 farmers with buyers across the country, helping to reduce food waste, lower prices for consumers, and increase farmer incomes by at least 40%.
