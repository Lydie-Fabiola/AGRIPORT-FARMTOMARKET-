How Our Pricing Works

At Farm2Market, we believe in keeping things simple and fair. We only make money when you make money.

3% Transaction Fee

We charge a small 3% fee only when you successfully sell your products. This fee helps us maintain the platform, provide customer support, and continue improving our services.

No Hidden Costs

There are no subscription fees, listing fees, or any other hidden charges. You can create an account, list your products, and connect with buyers completely free.

Payment Processing

The 3% fee includes payment processing costs. We handle the secure transfer of funds between buyers and farmers, ensuring that transactions are safe and reliable.

Transparent Earnings

Your dashboard clearly shows your earnings before and after the fee is applied. You'll always know exactly how much you're making from each sale.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do I pay the 3% fee?

+

The 3% fee is automatically deducted from the payment when a buyer purchases your products. You don't need to pay anything upfront or manually.

Are there any monthly or annual fees?

+

No, there are no subscription fees of any kind. You only pay when you make a sale.

How and when do I receive my payments?

+

Payments are processed within 48 hours after a buyer confirms receipt of the products. You can withdraw your earnings to your mobile money account or bank account at any time.

Is there a limit to how many products I can list?

+

No, you can list as many products as you want without any additional cost.

Do you offer any premium features?

+

Currently, all features are available to all users under our standard plan. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core services will always remain accessible to everyone.
