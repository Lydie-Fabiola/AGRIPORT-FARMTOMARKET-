Buying Products

Finding Products

Use filters to find exactly what you're looking for. You can search by:

  • Product type (vegetables, fruits, dairy, etc.)
  • Distance from your location
  • Organic certification
  • Price range

Reserving Items

When you find products you want, you can reserve them immediately. This guarantees availability until pickup or delivery.

Delivery Options

Choose between pickup at the farm, local markets, or home delivery where available.

Secure Payments

All transactions are processed securely. We support multiple payment methods including credit cards and mobile money.

If products don't meet the described quality, you're eligible for a refund or replacement.
