Creating a Strong Profile

Your profile is your digital storefront. Make it stand out with:

  • Clear photos of your farm
  • Your farming story and experience
  • Farming practices (organic, traditional, etc.)
  • Location and delivery options

Buyers are more likely to purchase from farmers with complete profiles they can trust.
